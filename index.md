This website is the culmination of a long-term wish to build a community where knowledge sharing is free and constructive.

I have been a telecom professional for the last 20 years and have learned a lot on the job. As a millennial, I have seen perhaps too many technological changes to fully master. I want to help fellow learning enthusiasts by providing an open-source platform of hands-on technical notes and insights.

This site documents the systems I study, design, and experiment with — covering both foundational principles and real-world implementation patterns. It serves as a continuously evolving archive of technical notes, architecture explorations, and system-building experiments.

My way of working aligns more with an individual contributor — or philosophically, a Silent Warrior who thrives on discipline, perseverance, and not giving up on himself. I occasionally write reflections on resilience, discipline, and inner growth for those navigating personal and professional crossroads.
